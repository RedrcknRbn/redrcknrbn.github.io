Agility

Agility is an enchantment that can be applied to boots, increasing the wearer's movement speed.

Obtaining Agility
  • Enchanting Table: Agility can be obtained through the Enchanting Table, similar to other enchantments. The cost increases with each level.
    • Minimum Level: 1 EXP Level (Increases by 11 per Book Level) 
    • Maximum Level: 25 EXP Levels (Increases by 9 per Book Level)
  • Anvil: You can combine existing enchanted boots or enchanted books with Agility on an anvil. The base cost for combining is 2 EXP Levels.
  • Villagers: Some Librarian villagers may offer Agility books as a trade.
  • Loot: Agility books can be found in various common loot chests, including those in structures.
How Agility Works

  • Speed Increase: Each level of Agility adds 0.2 to the player's Generic Movement Speed attribute. This translates to an increase of 3.44 blocks per second per enchantment level.
  • Description: The enchantment description is "I'm fast, too fast."
