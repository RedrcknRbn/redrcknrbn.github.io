Personality Info

For clarification's sake -

I have a personality disorder, where, depending on the day, I'll either be one of the few below.

THIS IS NOT THE SAME AS DID. I am me, just my personality and identity changes!

My Discord Status will change depending on my personality, formatted like "(Personality) Status"


Many things can trigger personality changes, so be aware that I may switch up during the day.

Red Alex Gray ?

Red is usually my main personality, and is the one you'll see me as the most.

Red is calm, usually collected, however, can be prone to anger issues.

While I'm Red, I don't have any preferred pronouns nor identity, so you can regard me as whatever you want!

Alex is usually the personality you'll see me as whenever I'm streaming, recording videos, or playing social games!

Alex is much more upbeat and energetic, however, with higher highs, comes lower lows, and thus is more prone to moodswings and sudden mood shifts.

Due to this, she usually doesn't come out for long, for a few hours at most.

While I'm Alex, my preferred pronouns are She/Her, and I often have a more feminine appearance.

Gray usually comes out after a moodswing from Alex, or during a rough day.

Gray is very quiet, and really only communicates through text. While Gray is out, it is usually physically exhausing to actually speak.

Gray often appears to be "depressive" or "angry", when in reality, they just don't want to communicate.

While I'm Gray, my preferred pronouns are usually They/Them.

Whenever you see my current personality listed as ?, this usually means I am going through mental issues, and am too unstable to stay one personality for long.

It's effectively an identifier for unknown personality, or an "unstable" personality.
