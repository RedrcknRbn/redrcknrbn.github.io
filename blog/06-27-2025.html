Where I am mentally

Written on June 27th, 2025

cw // suicide, mental health, obviously.


hello

for those wondering where i am rn, thats why i wrote this mini-blog post.

i am, uh, not doing good mentally, whatsoever

like its getting really bad, near my levels of 2017 depression (where i made multiple attempts on my life)

i wont ever try to kill myself again, but my brain keeps begging me to do it, so please dont worry about me ending my life


a lot is happening right now that i cant process

i may have to ask for donations once again for bills

my life keeps getting worse due to bills and legislation being passed

and other personal reasons


i usually try to keep my mental health rants on my vent bluesky account, but i feel as though people shouldnt have to scour the internet to find out whats happening


anyways, uh,

i usually try to keep a happy facade up 24/7, and i pretend to be joyful, when in reality, it couldnt be further from the truth

each and every day that passes is a neverending nightmare of misery, and each night i hope i never wake up, that this is my final night on this earth

i know this would harm others around me if i were to suddenly die or disappear, but it would be peace for me

how fucking selfish of me to even consider such a thing


this is kinda scatterbrained, i dont mean it to be, but im just typing out my actual, raw emotions right now

whatever comes to my brain, im typing, so this isnt in any particular order


i know there are some people counting on me, but they should be relying on others

why fucking rely on me? why hope for my wellbeing when there are other, better people out there

other people you can try to save

other people that are better

stronger

i barely function as is

and you consider me worthwhile?

fuck that, man


anyways uhm

idk whats going on with me

idk if im gonna take a break

i feel like i cant though, due to said people

i have to keep communicating

otherwise i go insane

but i need some way to relieve steam

some way to release my stress

idk how /shrug

i mean i know one way how but thats the final solution lol


anyways uh, i hope youre doing good if youre reading this

please, dont end up like me

dont get so deep in everything to where you cant breathe

ive got like 20 different things going on that are piling on top of me, and im starting to crack

dont be like me

please
