The Countdown

Written on August 20th, 2025

Heres a log all of the Countdown Days, keep in mind, the Countdown does not represent actual days, it represents something else.


- 14 Days -

desire unfufilled, heartbreak past

for there is nobody to help me now

nor will there be anybody to help me later

do i continue in pain, or do i conclude in peace?

for those that will miss me are fooling themselves

i am not needed. let me end in peace.


- 13 Days -

if one is shattered, how must one come back together

to form a union, a pact

one piece of the glass, missing

unable to form the whole yet again

if a system i know has shattered

with a missing piece

how may i help them

help them all find peace


- 12 Days -

when the silence is deafening

where must one turn to?

there is nothing for me now

all but void does not remain

the darkness gets darker

as each day passes

how must one cope

with the lack of light?


- 11 Days -

when the pain is great

and I cant move on

what place do I go to

besides the end?


- 10 Days -

when one whole is ripped apart

and theres nobody to hold it together

then i shatter

with nothing to stabilize me

i cant handle the pieces

and i need someone to help me

and yet, theres nobody

im deemed nothing to be


- 9 Days -

those that hold me together

i couldnt thank enough

despite how much i tell them

i still feel ungrateful

like i didnt earn their support

like they should let me fall

oh how i think about how it would be like

if i werent here


- 8 Days -

each and every day that passes

one more tear escapes my eyes

and for what reason?

what reason besides torture?

the infinite abyss calls me

do I accept its call to fall,

or do I remain to be tortured daily?


- 7 Days -

misfortune

misdeeds

yet no punishment

only harming the innocent


- 6 Days -

i give myself a false form to attempt to better myself for others

i give myself a false hope that others will notice

i give myself a false motivation to keep going, only for others

i give myself a false face to keep others happy

i do this

not for myself

but for them


- 5 Days -

the mask strengthens my form

keeps me sane

structured

when the mask collapses

i let it all out

and god willing i do not collapse


- 4 Days - 

a trauma revealing myself

lightness of senses

shortness of breath

contending with death

i shall remain


- 3 Days -

its all downhill

everyone i know

including myself

we are all falling now

with nobody to catch us

in freefall we scramble

and struggle to remain

soon

there will be nothing but red paint to mark us now


- 2 Days -

shattered,

gone,

i harmed myself,

and i regret it.

i have nothing to cover myself now.

besides pain

and disaster


- 1 Day -

I started this as a coping mechanism

A cry for help

A hope for a better now

But I see clear as day

There was no hope

It was words in a void

Shouting out

Hoping someone would hear them
