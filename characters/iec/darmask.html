The Darmask

IEC-448 (Rioso below)

The Darmask, (Codename IEC-448), is this skin-like, impossibly smooth, oily yet rubbery mask that contorts based on it's emotions. 

The texture of The Darmask is described to be similar to oily skin. 

The Darmask has the ability to form a body. This process has never been observed, and we do not know how it occurs. 

When The Darmask forms a body, it develops a unique personality, tailored to the body it has created. Any bodies it creates do not contain heads. The mask remains suspended where the face of the body would normally be. 

The Darmask rarely speaks, but when it does, the voice that it has is raspy, quiet, and breathy. Small gusts of hot air emanate from the mouth area when it speaks.

The Darmask has the ability to change it's "eye" color when it has a body, meaning, when a body is formed, the eye sockets can have red light glowing from them.

The Darmask is slow. Calculating. Always thinking. Despite this, in every personality it forms, it has an exploitable ego, thinking itself better than others.


The Darmask's current form, [[Rioso]] is extremely risky to capture, and thus, the [[Institution of Entity Containment]] is only merely observing it.

Rioso

Rioso is [[IEC-448 (The Darmask)]]'s current form.

Rioso is a Mafioso for the biggest underground crime family, the Buil Family.

Rioso exclusively wears a black and white pinstripe outfit, with a black and white pinstripe fedora and white gloves Rioso appears to have black hair

Rioso has a slightly cocky and over the top demeanor, attempting to look "cool" in every situation it can

[[IEC-448 (The Darmask)]] has taken on the form of Rioso, the right hand for the Buil Family. Not much is known about why [[IEC-448 (The Darmask)]] has taken on this form. It is speculated to be to avoid capture.

Rioso's current form is extremely risky to capture, and thus, the [[Institution of Entity Containment]] is only merely observing it.
