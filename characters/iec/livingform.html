The Living Form

IEC-604

The Living Form, commonly known as IEC-604, is a living Social Security SSA-16 Disability form. It is currently contained at the [[I.E.C. Primary Containment Facility]]. IEC-604 is an entity that was hiding within the Social Security Administration building in New York. It would replace an SSA-16 form being shipped out to households, taking the place of it. Upon being filled out and sent back to the SSA building, IEC-604 would copy the recipient's handwriting and replace text within the form to make the recipient ineligible for Social Security Disability benefits. It is currently unknown how many forms have been altered by IEC-604, but the current estimate is in the tens of thousands.
